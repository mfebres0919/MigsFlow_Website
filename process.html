FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build my website?

On average, most websites are completed within 1 week. If your project is more complex or requires additional features, it may take up to 2 weeks. For basic 1–3 page websites, 1 week is my max.

What do I need to provide you to get started?

I’ll need your business name, logo, photos, and any written content you want on your pages. I’ll also ask a few quick questions during your free consultation to get a clear vision for the design.

What if I don’t like the design?

I offer a free homepage preview before you commit. If you’re not happy with the direction, you won’t be charged — no pressure.

Can you help me buy a domain?

Yes! If you don’t already have a domain, I can help you purchase one and get it set up with your website.

Do you offer hosting?

Yes — all my monthly plans include secure and reliable website hosting, so you won’t have to worry about finding a separate provider.

Do you offer website updates or maintenance?

Yes! My monthly plan includes 2 updates per month (like swapping photos, updating text, etc.). If you’re not on a plan, you can still request updates for a flat fee.

Can I edit the site myself later?

These websites are hand coded myself and are built for performance and stability. If you ever need changes, just reach out — I’ll take care of it for you so you don’t have to deal with the technical stuff.

Do you offer refunds?

I aim to make sure every client is happy with their website. That’s why I offer a free consultation and homepage preview before any payment is required. Once a project has been approved and development is underway, I don’t offer full refunds. However, if you're unsatisfied during the early phase of the project, we can discuss a partial refund based on the work completed up to that point.

Why hire me instead of using Squarespace, Webflow, Framer, or other website builers?

Website builders often slow sites down and limit flexibility. I hand-code using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which means your site loads faster, runs smoother, and is fully tailored to your brand. Plus, I include basic SEO setup so your site is search-engine ready from day one.

How do I send you everything you need for the website?

Once we start the project, I’ll send you a Google Form where you can easily upload all your content — like photos, text, and business info — in one place. It’s simple, organized, and keeps the process smooth.
