Frequently Asked Questions

Why does mobile optimization matter for my website?

Over half of all web traffic comes from mobile devices. If your site isn’t mobile-optimized, you risk losing visitors, conversions, and ranking lower on Google.

Will my existing website work better on phones after optimization?

Yes. I’ll improve layout, spacing, image scaling, and load times so your site looks sharp and functions smoothly across all screen sizes.

Does mobile optimization improve SEO?

Absolutely. Google prioritizes mobile-friendly websites in search results, so optimizing your site can boost visibility and traffic.

Will this affect how my site works on tablets too?

Yes. I design and test across multiple devices, including tablets, to ensure your site is responsive at every size.
